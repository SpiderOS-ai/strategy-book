### Odoo-Specific Customization Guidance

- Odoo offers **100+ base modules** including Accounting, Inventory, Project, Sales, Purchase, MRP, HR, POS, CRM, eCommerce, Website, Marketing Automation
- Best practice: adapt business processes to standard Odoo flows first; customize only when configuration cannot meet critical requirements
- Odoo Studio allows no-code customization; Python/XML for advanced needs
- Even at 80% satisfaction with out-of-box, the missing 20% may be critical enough to require customization
- Small companies (<50 employees): work directly with Odoo using Success Packs
- Mid-size companies (>50 employees): work with a local partner
- Large companies (250+ employees): contact Odoo directly
- Phased approach recommended: go live with standard functionality first, add customizations based on real usage

## 8. E-INVOICING 2026 MANDATE (FACTURATION ELECTRONIQUE)

### Timeline

- **September 1, 2026:** All large enterprises and ETIs must be able to **receive** electronic invoices
- **September 1, 2027:** Must also **emit** electronic invoices and transmit invoicing data to the administration (e-reporting)
- Complete dematerialization of inter-enterprise invoices becomes a legal obligation for all businesses

### Odoo's Readiness

- Odoo has supported **Factur-X generation** for many years
- Automatic reception of supplier invoices available
- **Peppol connection** available since Odoo 17
- Odoo can produce structured formats: **Factur-X** and **UBL-based formats**
- Module for PDP (Plateforme de Dematerialisation Partenaire) connection available
- Integrated automation and traceability features

### Strategic Implications

- The mandate is driving ERP adoption and upgrades among French SMBs
- Cegid/EBP acquisition explicitly positioned to capture this market
- Odoo partners positioning e-invoicing readiness as a key selling point
- This represents an opportunity for SMBs to automate accounting processes and secure financial workflows
