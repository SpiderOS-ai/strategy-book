### 3.7 Palantir Reverse Engineering / Bootcamp Model

**Nathan** has conducted ~30-40 deep research sessions across PerplexityAI, Claude, and ChatGPT to reverse-engineer Palantir's operating model. He organized findings into four axes:
1. How they manage their sales pipeline
2. How they manage the first 90-day pilot project
3. How they manage Expand & Scale
4. How they staff and structure teams

**The Bootcamp model:** Palantir offers free 5-day bootcamps at client sites with 10-15 client participants. Two weeks before, they request data access and prepare a demo. Day 1 they show "here's what we built with your data." Then collaborative workshops.

> "Ils vont dans une boite, font un bootcamp de 5 jours avec en gros 10 a 15 recolteurs dans la boite [...] premier jour ils montrent en disant voila on a travaille avec un visage voila a quoi ca pourrait ressembler." [VERBATIM -- original attribution: Nathan, 2:40:59]

**Thierry** immediately recognizes the parallel with what he already does at Airbus and with Drakkar's discovery process (TBA, RGMC examples). He describes his own method: launch 3-4 short workshops on real operational pain points, solve them in 15 days, which creates cognitive buy-in for the larger transformation vision.

> "Je lance 3-4 workshops de 15 jours avec des specialistes sur des sujets pays foule pour eux [...] je resouds des problemes mais a travers les problemes que je resouds du quotidien je cree des biais cognitifs aupres des equipes ou je leur montre qu'il est possible de maitriser son changement."
