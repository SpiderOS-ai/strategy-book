## KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR LLM

The 10 theses in order, with one-sentence summaries:

1. **These 1 -- Le tacite est le dernier tresor:** Tacit knowledge (relational, craft, bespoke) is the only non-replicable asset European SMEs possess, and it is dying with every retirement and failed succession.

2. **These 2 -- La vente B2B est un mensonge:** B2B sales in Europe run on visceral trust built over decades, not on CRM pipelines -- CRMs capture theater, not reality.

4. **These 4 -- Ils veulent la couronne, pas le fardeau:** European SME leaders (family owners, corporate acquirers, heirs) all want the title of boss without the burden of governing -- so tacit knowledge dies because nobody pays the price to save it.

5. **These 5 -- Le ratio est catastrophique:** Every existing solution (CRM, ERP, consultants) demands public admission of weakness, which European leaders will never accept -- Spider inverts the ratio by requiring zero effort and zero admission.
